About


xaround is a social networking app designed specifically for college and university communities. Traditional campus confession platforms like Facebook Pages come with their own limitations - from privacy concerns in the comment section to inefficient content reposting. xaround provides a solution to these challenges, offering a tailored environment for campus communities.


Our app stands unique with its offer of both identified and anonymous participation, ensuring freedom of expression in a safe, respectful setting. Crafted with native iOS and Android design, xaround delivers an easy-to-navigate, user-friendly experience.

xaround is more than just a confession platform. It fosters intriguing discussions on a myriad of topics, enriches academic life, and creates a sense of unity. Users have the freedom to explore happenings not only on their own campus but also on others, broadening their perspectives.

Our app supports a variety of post types including text, link, and image (including GIF). Additionally, users have the ability to save posts for later reference. Beyond being a networking app, xaround is a one-stop solution aimed at making campus life more vibrant and connected. In our quest to build the perfect platform, we invite users to participate in app development discussions through an exclusive in-app forum.
